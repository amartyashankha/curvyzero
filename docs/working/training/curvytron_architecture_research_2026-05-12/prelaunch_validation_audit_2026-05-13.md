## Prelaunch Read

The two newly launch-relevant features, `survival_plus_bonus_no_outcome` and
`blank_canvas_noop`, have focused local coverage and passed the bounded checks.
I did not find a compile-breaking bug or a small isolated test gap that justified
editing code/tests during this audit.

Updated handoff: the tiny stock-trainer canary gate has passed for
`blank_canvas_noop/body_circles_fast`, `blank_canvas_noop/browser_lines`, and
`normal/body_circles_fast` after the shared-support fix. Treat the proactive
wall-avoidant opponent as a probe/design artifact only until it has first-class
trainer plumbing and tests.
