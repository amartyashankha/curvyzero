Glossary: default `track_ball` tracks the current ball row. In the current
default geometry it is a survival/tie floor, because exact search found no
normal-reset scoring sequence against it within the cap; that does not prove
`track_ball` is optimal in all Pong variants. `lagged_track_ball_1` tracks the
previous ball row, which adds one tick of lag and makes the target scoreable
from normal resets. Shaped proxy means the separate loss-delay diagnostic or
training target, not a replacement reward. Pong summaries should report wins,
mean/median survival steps, truncation rate, opponent wins/losses, and shaped
proxy together. Do not reduce learned-vs-`track_ball` back to a bare fraction.
Variance is not an environment reward term, but it is allowed as a small
bounded exploration or checkpoint-selection signal. Log survival standard
deviation, p90 survival, max survival, rare wins, and shaped-return standard
deviation. If two policies have similar mean score/survival, the one with a
wider useful tail may be more promising. Do not let this promote noisy
stalling or policies that regress against `random_uniform`.
